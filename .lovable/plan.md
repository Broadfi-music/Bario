# Bario Investor Pitch Deck — Generation Plan

## Design Philosophy: "Signal Noir"

A dark, confident, minimal aesthetic. Deep blacks with emerald green accents (matching Bario's brand). Clean typography, generous whitespace, product screenshots as the primary visual evidence. No decorative fluff. Every element earns its place.

## What This Fixes From The Old Deck

- Removes "Interactive Layer for Audio Culture" (too technical)
- Removes duplicate slides (Differentiation + USP + Competitive Edge = one slide)
- Focuses on ONE revenue model (gifting platform fee)
- Shows real product screenshots as proof the MVP works
- Uses plain, human language throughout
- Positions as global platform (not Africa-specific)
- Reduces competitors to 3

## Slide Structure (10 slides)

1. **Cover** — "BARIO" + "Go Live. Earn Money." + product screenshot
2. **Problem** — People listen to audio alone. Creators can't earn from it. (2 clear pain points)
3. **Solution** — Live audio rooms where fans send gifts, creators cash out real money
4. **How It Works** — 3 steps with product screenshots: Go Live → Receive Gifts → Cash Out
5. **The Product** — Full product screenshots (homepage, live rooms, AI remix) proving the MVP exists
6. **Market** — $23B podcast + $250B creator economy convergence
7. **Why Us** — 3 competitors max (Twitter Spaces, Spotify, TikTok) comparison table
8. **Traction** — Real numbers: 200 users, 27K TikTok, 7K Instagram, live product at bario.app
9. **Revenue** — Platform fee on gifting (NOW), creator subscriptions (SOON)
10. **Team + Ask** — 4 team members, raising $20K for 12-18 months runway

## Technical Approach

- Generate as PDF using ReportLab
- Landscape orientation (standard pitch deck format)
- Embed actual product screenshots from the live app
- Use Outfit font (available in canvas fonts) for modern feel
- Color palette: #0A0A0A (background), #22C55E (Bario green accent), #FFFFFF (text)
- Capture 3 product screenshots as evidence images
