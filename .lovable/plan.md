## Slide Order (12 slides — lean, no fluff)

1. **Cover** — "BARIO" / "Go Live. Build Fans. Earn Money." / tagline only, no clutter
2. **The Problem** — Creators have audiences but no way to monetize live conversations. Twitter Spaces = no revenue. Clubhouse = dead. TikTok Live = video-only.
3. **The Solution** — Live audio rooms where fans send virtual gifts. Creators earn real money. Simple.
4. **The Product** — 2 polished product shots (Live Feed + Inside a Room). Proves it's built, not a concept.
5. **How It Works** — 3-step flow: Go Live → Fans Send Gifts → Creator Earns (30% platform fee)
6. **AI Remix (The Moat)** — Brief: AI-powered music remixing adds a unique creative layer. Screenshot of the remix hero. This is what makes Bario different — not just talk, but create.
7. **Traction** — "Small Numbers, Big Signal": 46 users, 117 sessions, 89 gifts, 2.54 sessions/user, 1.93 gifts/user. Compare to industry benchmarks.
8. **Market** — Creator economy = $250B. Live audio = $31B. Our beachhead: African creators going global. SAM: $15M.
9. **Competition** — 3 competitors only: Twitter Spaces (no monetization), TikTok Live (video-only), Clubhouse (dead). Bario = audio + gifting + AI.
10. **Revenue Model** — One model: 30% cut on all virtual gifts. Simple unit economics.
11. **3-Year Financial Projection** — Y1: 1K creators, $2K MRR / Y2: 10K creators, $25K MRR / Y3: 50K creators, $150K MRR. Conservative and believable for a $20k raise.
12. **The Ask** — $20,000 pre-seed. Use of funds: Complete MVP (40%), First 1,000 creators (30%), Infrastructure (30%). Contact info.
